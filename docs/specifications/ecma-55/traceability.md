## Coverage Notes

- The requirement corpus currently focuses on normative behavior, parser obligations, runtime semantics, and documentation duties.
- Table 1 and Table 2 are only partially legible in OCR. Their practical requirements are represented through Section 4 character-set rules and Appendix 4 documentation requirements, but those two table pages should still be checked manually against the PDF before claiming formal conformance coverage.
- Appendix 1 and Appendix 2 are used as interpretation aids for structure and syntax notation. They are intentionally not expanded into many standalone requirements because they mostly explain how to read the standard rather than defining executable language behavior.
